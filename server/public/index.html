How It Works

  1. Show the AI how you're communicating with your partner. Just copy and paste a sample of your conversations from text or email
  2. The AI will review each message, tag it with some helpful commentary, and then generate a detailed report summarizing the nature of the exchange, what it says about your relationship, and advice for both partners to communicate better.
  3. Chat with the AI and ask questions about the analysis or other aspects of the relationship
  4. Share the results with your partner (if you want to). Your session is totally private and confidential, and can only be viewed with your explicit permission

Features and Benefits

  • Deep Analysis: Understand your communication patterns beyond the surface, and the emotional tone of your partner's responses.
  • Resolve Conflicts: Highlight toxic or unproductive statements made by you and your partner, and track the health of your interactions.
  • Practical Advice: Learn how to express yourself in ways that will be well received by your partner.
  • No Limits: The initial report is just the beginning. Explore the complexities of your relationship in as much depth as you want, through open ended conversation with the AI.
